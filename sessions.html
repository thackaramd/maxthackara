Sessions

I offer one-to-one conversations where you can think things through out loud and arrive at a clearer sense of where you stand.

Things often become more settled—and more straightforward—than they first appear.

*

People are already coherent.

Most of the time, something simply becomes clouded—in thinking, in language, or in how a situation is being held.

You can be wrong about something without anything being wrong with you. You can also be misunderstood without that making you incoherent.

The conversation is a way of staying with what is already there long enough for it to become clearer and more workable in language.

I listen closely, work with what you’re saying, and help refine how it is being held—sometimes by reflecting something back, sometimes by questioning it, sometimes by staying with it a little longer than you would on your own.

I don’t offer advice or try to direct you toward a particular outcome.

Clarity tends to emerge through the process itself—not as a sudden insight, but as something that gradually becomes more precise and stable as we stay with it.

You don’t need to prepare anything. You don’t need to present yourself in a particular way. You can begin exactly where you are.

*

These conversations tend to suit people who are not looking to be advised, coached, or fixed, but who want space to think carefully and speak without pressure, in a way that actually holds.

They are not a substitute for clinical or therapeutic support.
