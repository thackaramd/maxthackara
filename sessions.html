Sometimes things don’t need fixing.
They just aren’t clear.

I don’t think people are broken.

Most of the time, something just gets clouded—
in thinking, in language, in how a situation is being held.

You can be wrong about something without anything being wrong with you.

I don’t offer advice or try to lead you anywhere.

We just talk.

I listen carefully, stay with what you’re saying, and help you find a way of putting things that actually fits.

Sometimes that means slowing things down.
Sometimes it means questioning something.
Sometimes it’s just staying with it a bit longer than you would on your own.

What tends to happen is that things become clearer.

Not because something new is added,
but because what’s already there starts to make sense again.

You don’t need to come with anything prepared.

You don’t need to be a certain way.

You can just start where you are.
