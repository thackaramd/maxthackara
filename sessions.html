Clarity Sessions

I offer one-to-one conversations where you can think things through out loud and arrive at a clearer sense of where you stand.

Often, things feel more settled—or more fine—than you might have expected.

My Position

I don’t think people are broken.

Most of the time, something just gets clouded—in thinking, in language, or in how a situation is being held.

You can be wrong about something without anything being wrong with you. You can also be misunderstood without that making you incoherent.

I pay close attention to what is actually real, and I stay with it.

What I Do

I don’t offer advice or try to lead you anywhere.

I listen carefully, stay with what you’re saying, and help you find a way of putting things that actually fits—sometimes by reflecting something back, sometimes by questioning it, sometimes by staying with it a little longer.

What Happens

We sit with what you bring and work through it together, in conversation.

That might mean slowing things down, questioning something, or staying with it a bit longer than you would on your own.

What tends to happen is that things become clearer—not because something new is added, but because what’s already there starts to make sense again.

You don’t need to come with anything prepared. You don’t need to be a certain way. You can just start where you are.

This kind of conversation isn’t for everyone. It may appeal if you’re not looking to be fixed or guided, but simply want space to think and speak without pressure.

It isn’t a substitute for more structured or clinical forms of support.
