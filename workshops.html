Creative Writing Workshops
As a qualified teacher and self-published author based in Bedford, I deliver creative writing workshops for primary and secondary students that inspire imagination, build confidence, and foster a genuine love of writing.

My workshops are calm, discussion-led, and tailored to each group. Students are encouraged to explore their own ideas, experiment with language, and develop their voice in a supportive, low-pressure environment.

I'm currently offering these workshops as a free, optional extra-curricular session for local schools. As availability is limited each term, do get in touch to see what might be possible.
