Creative Writing Workshops
I offer creative writing workshops for primary and secondary students, designed to encourage imagination, build confidence, and develop a genuine enjoyment of writing.

Sessions are calm, discussion-based, and responsive to each group. Students are supported in exploring their own ideas, experimenting with language, and developing their voice in a low-pressure, engaging environment.
Primary Workshops
  • KS1–KS2 (ages 5–11)
  • Playful, imaginative approach to storytelling
  • Focus on building confidence and enjoyment in writing
  • Exploration of ideas through discussion and creative prompts
  • Flexible activities shaped around the group
